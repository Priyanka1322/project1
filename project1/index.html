Lata Mangeshkar

1929-2022

The melody of india

Lata Mangeshkar was an Indian Marathi playback singer and occasional music composer. She is considered to be the most celebrated singer in independent India and one of its most influential artists. Her voice was one of the unifying elements of the peoples of South Asia across borders.
